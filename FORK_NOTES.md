## About Delex

Delex is a DAG-based multi-strategy blocking system for entity matching. It
allows multiple blocking heuristics — including TF/IDF, rule-based, and
custom strategies — to be composed into a single blocking pipeline, with
configurable fallback and combination semantics.

## Active Development
